About me

I am Ahmed, a seasoned individual whose roots trace back to the vibrant city of Alexandria, Egypt. My journey has led me across continents, from my formative years spent in the dynamic atmosphere of Houston, Texas, to my current abode in the serene landscapes of the Pacific Northwest, specifically Portland, Oregon. With a profound passion for technology and a vision of myself as a future software developer, I have strategically immersed myself in the realm of Information Technology. My current involvement within the IT department has proven to be an invaluable asset, offering me a unique vantage point and a wealth of skills that I am eager to channel into my forthcoming career. Amidst the professional strides, I have nurtured a rich tapestry of hobbies that color the canvas of my life. Among these, photography stands as a testament to my keen eye for detail, capturing fleeting moments and preserving them as timeless artifacts. Equally drawn to the allure of the great outdoors, I find solace in hiking through rugged terrains and embarking on expeditions to uncover the hidden wonders of nature. In summation, my narrative is one of dynamic movement, cultivated expertise, and an unwavering enthusiasm for both the digital realm and the natural world. This juxtaposition of passions fuels my ambitions and promises a future brimming with accomplishment and discovery.
